Fake News Binary Classification

We explored many models and found out that fake news authors like to yell a lot.

  • TFIDF-Trained XGBoost
  • Numerically-Trained XGBoost
  • TFIDF DNN
This model trains an XGBoost Binary Classifier on extracted TF-IDF data from the concatenated text and title of the article.
It restricts the extracted vocabulary to broader terms that appear more frequently between all documents to keep the model as flexible
as possible while maximizing accuracy.
This model trains an XGBoost Binary Classifier on extracted numerical data from the text and title of the article,
such as percent of uppercase words, sentiment scores, and text or title length. Much of this numerical data is collected
is collected from a version of the text that have been both lemmatized and had its stop words removed,
although some features are extracted from the raw text, such as percent of uppercase words.
